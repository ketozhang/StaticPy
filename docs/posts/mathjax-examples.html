MathJax Examples

Last updated 2019-05-02 03:48:12 PM

MathJax is a mathematics web display engine most popular for LaTeX. Here are some typical examples of MathJax support for LaTeX syntax.

Inline Math

Newton's second law in simple scalar form is \(F=ma\).

Display Math

Newton's second law in simple scalar form is,

\[ F = ma \]

Environment Math

Equation

\[ \begin{equation} F = ma \end{equation} \]

Align

\[ \begin{align} F &= ma\\ \dot p &= m \dot a \end{align} \]

Gather

\[ \begin{gather} F = ma\\ \frac{\mathrm d p}{\mathrm d x} = m \frac{\mathrm d^2 x}{\mathrm d t^2} \end{gather} \]
