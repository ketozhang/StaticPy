A = USVT

  • V : Orthogonal matrix of M × M
  • S : Rank diagonal matrix of m × m
  • U : Column orthogonal matrix of n × m

Rank Diagonal Matrix

S is a diagonal matrix limited by its rank. This means that the number of nonzero entries are determined by the rank of A (let r = rank(A))


$$ S = \begin{pmatrix} \lambda_1 & 0 & \ldots & 0\\ 0 & \lambda_2 & \ldots & 0\\ \vdots & 0 & \ddots & 0\\ & & & \lambda_r\\ & & & & 0 \end{pmatrix} $$
